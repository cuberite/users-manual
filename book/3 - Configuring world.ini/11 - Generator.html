Specifies setting for the generator of this world. This is the most powerful tool when customizing worlds.

When modifying this please be aware that already generated worlds won't connect seamlessly to the newly generated terrain. Also keep in mind that worlds created from other generators (e.g. the notchian generator) won't connect seamlessly either!
The choice of generator will highly impact the other choices you'll have afterwards.
